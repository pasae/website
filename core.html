Angelo Braganza

Co-External President

Mechanical Engineering, 2023
he/him/his

Hi, I’m Angelo Braganza, a third-year mechanical engineering major and one of Core 34’s External Presidents. My responsibilities range from maintaining company and alumni relations, representing PASAE in greater the Pilipinx community, and preparing professional development resources for our members. I love to see my fellow PASAE members succeed in their academic and professional endeavors, helping them develop the necessary skills and experience needed to land their dream internship or job.

Outside of PASAE I help build solar cars with CalSol, conduct academic research at on-campus labs, and dip my toes into other Pilipinx organizations on campus. In my free time I enjoy urban and landscape photography, swimming at Spieker, and going all out for Cal Game Days.

Professionally, I want to pursue an MBA and work in product research and development. Feel free to reach out to me to talk about all things PASAE or non-PASAE related!
