Anthony Magsarili

Social Chair

MCB: Immunology, 2022
he/him/his

Hi, I am AJ Magsarili and I am PASAE's social chair. My job is to build up the community of PASAE by planning social events that foster deep friendships, inspire collaboration, and create an inclusive and fun environment. Being a social chair allows me a unique perspective as I work in both the creative and management realms. Social events require creativity: coming up with new and unique events that can capture the attention of our diverse community and to keep things fresh for returning members. Working as the social chair also requires keen planning and logistical skills: making a clear vision of what you want to accomplish, how to enact it, and executing no matter what obstacles you may encounter.

Outside of PASAE, I am a member of Phi Delta Epsilon pre-medical fraternity. There I am the resource manager and connect our members to unique medical-related positions and opportunities as well as organize our large academic resource bank. In addition, I am a member of Pi Kappa Phi social fraternity where I serve as the Philanthropy chair: there I provide aid and attention to people with developmental disabilities. I also participate in various other clubs and campus and if you are interested in me, what I do in PASAE, or what I do outside of PASAE please feel free to reach out to me personally!

Fun Fact: I'm color blind.
